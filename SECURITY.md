# Security Vulnerability Reporting Policy

## Introduction
We take security vulnerabilities seriously and want to ensure they are properly reported and addressed.

## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you believe you've found a security vulnerability in this project, please report it to us immediately by sending an email to [security@jobifyy.com](mailto:security@jobifyy.com).

Please include the following information in your report:
- A clear and concise description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Any screenshots or proofs of concept that may assist us in understanding the issue.

## Response Time
We aim to acknowledge your report within 72 hours and will keep you updated on our progress as we investigate and address the issue.

## Disclosure Policy
We appreciate your efforts to improve the security of our project and will make every effort to appropriately credit your contributions. We commit to not disclosing any vulnerabilities publicly until they have been fixed. Thank you for helping us keep this project secure!
